Fresh & Flavourful Quinoa Salad

Looking for a light, nutritious, and delicious meal? This quinoa salad is the perfect choice! Packed with vibrant veggies, protein-rich quinoa, and a tangy lemon dressing, itâ€™s a refreshing dish that works as a side or a satisfying main course.

Ingredients

  • 200 g cooked quinoa, cooled
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 100 g cucumber, diced
  • 50 g red bell pepper, chopped
  • 50 g chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced or diced
  • 30 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 fresh parsley sprigs, chopped

Dressing

  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 5 ml honey or maple syrup
  • 2.5 g salt
  • 1 g black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, chickpeas, and avocado.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  4. Sprinkle with feta cheese and fresh parsley.
  5. Serve immediately or chill for 15 minutes for more flavor.
